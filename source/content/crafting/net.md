## Necessary Materials

*   **Knife** (Tool for woodworking)
*   **Grass** (Abundant quantities required)
*   **Wood** (For the frame)

## Item Overview

The **Fishing Net** is an advanced tool used for aquatic harvesting. Unlike a simple fishing rod, the net allows for the simultaneous capture of multiple fish, significantly increasing your resource gathering efficiency.

## Complex Assembly Instructions

The construction of a fishing net is a labor-intensive process involving multiple stages of refinement.

### Phase 1: Textile Production
1.  **String Creation**: Equipping **Grass** in both hands and pressing **T** will yield a **String**. You must produce a total of **400 Strings**.
2.  **Tied Strings**: Combine two **Strings** to form a **Tied String**. You require **80 Tied Strings**.
3.  **Heavy Reinforcement**: Combine two **Tied Strings** to form a **Heavily Tied String**. You require **16 Heavily Tied Strings**.
4.  **Woven Strands**: Combine two **Heavily Tied Strings** to form a **Woven String**. You require **8 Woven Strings**.
5.  **Net Fabric**: Progressively combine woven components:
    - 2x **Woven String** -> **Heavily Woven String** (Need 4)
    - 2x **Heavily Woven String** -> **Half Net** (Need 2)
    - 2x **Half Net** -> **Net Without Frame** (Final mesh)

### Phase 2: Frame Construction
1.  **Woodworking**: With a **Knife** in one hand and **Wood** in the other, press **T** to produce **Carved Wood**. You require **6 units**.
2.  **Shaft Fabrication**: Use the **Knife** on **Carved Wood** to create a **Shaft**. You require **4 Shafts**.
3.  **Structural Support**: 
    - Combine 2x **Shaft** -> **Long Shaft** (Need 2)
    - Combine **Long Shaft** + **Carved Wood** -> **Half Frame** (Need 2)
    - Combine 2x **Half Frame** -> **Full Frame**

### Phase 3: Final Integration
1.  **Assembly**: Equip the **Full Frame** in one hand and the **Net Without Frame** in the other. Press **T** to complete your **Fishing Net**.
