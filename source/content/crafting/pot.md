## Necessary Materials

*   **Clay** (x2)

## Item Overview

The **Clay Pot** is a durable container designed for water storage. In the future, it will also serve as an essential vessel for culinary preparations, such as cooking noodles.

## Crafting Instructions

1.  **Gather Materials**: Obtain two units of **Clay**.
2.  **Molding**: Equip one piece of **Clay** in each hand.
3.  **Formation**: Press **T** to mold the clay into a finished **Clay Pot**.
