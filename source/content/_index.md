# Welcome to the Official Terra Wilds Portal

Step into a world of survival, exploration, and endless possibilities. Terra Wilds offers a unique blend of adventure and life-simulation mechanics, tailored for those who dare to forge their own path.

## Core Information

*   **Project Name:** Terra Wilds
*   **Genre:** Multiplayer Survival / Adventure
*   **Developer:** Technology Entertainment Team

## The World of Terra Wilds

In this realm, you are the master of your destiny. Whether you choose to be a legendary explorer mapping uncharted territories, a master craftsman supplying the community, or a humble fisherman enjoying the coastal breeze, every decision shapes your journey.

### Survival Mechanics
Experience a deep attribute system that challenges your management skills:
- **Vital Signs**: Monitor Hunger, Thirst, and Sickness.
- **Environmental Impact**: Your surroundings affect your well-being (e.g., increased thirst in desert regions).
- **Personal Hygiene**: Keep track of dirtiness and its effects on your health.

## Key Features

*   **Dynamic Raffle System**: Test your luck for a chance to win rare items, level boosts, and random surprises.
*   **Expansive Map System**: Traverse vast, diverse landscapes each with its own secrets to uncover.
*   **Deep Item Economy**: Hunt for rare artifacts and trade valuable resources with other survivors.
*   **Primitive Survival**: Master the hunting and gathering system. Dissect prey and utilize every resource to stay alive.
