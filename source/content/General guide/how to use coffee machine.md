The **Coffee Machine** is a specialized device used to brew various types of coffee and lattes. Proper operation ensures the best quality beverage and maintains the longevity of the machine.

## Brewing Instructions

Follow these steps to prepare your cup of coffee:

1.  **Retrieve the Machine**: Press **I** to open your backpack. Locate the **Coffee Machine** and equip it to either your **Left Hand** or **Right Hand**. Press **Escape** to conclude.
2.  **Deployment**: On the main screen, navigate to the hand holding the machine and press **Space** to place it on the ground and activate it.
3.  **Add Ingredients (Coffee/Espresso)**: Open your backpack (**I**), select either **Coffee** or **Espresso**, and equip it. Press **Escape** to exit. While standing by the machine, press **Space** to add the ingredient.
4.  **Add Liquid (Milk/Water)**:
    - **Using Milk**: Equip **Milk** and add it to the machine using **Space**, following the same procedure as the coffee.
    - **Using Water**: If you prefer water, equip a **Water Bottle**. Press **T** to open the bottle (ensure your other hand is empty). Equip the **Open Water Bottle** and add it to the machine using **Space**.
5.  **Initiate Brewing**: Press **D** to start the process. You will hear the machine operating. Wait until the sound ceases completely, indicating the cycle is finished.
6.  **Harvesting**: Open your backpack (**I**) and equip a **Cup**. Navigate to the hand holding the cup and press **Space** to catch the freshly brewed coffee.

> [!CAUTION]
> You **must** have a cup ready to collect the coffee. If no cup is present, the brewed beverage will be lost. Cups are crafted items.

7.  **Post-Process Maintenance**: Press **Enter** to retrieve the coffee machine back into your backpack. 

> [!WARNING]
> Failing to return the machine to your backpack immediately will result in it becoming dirty, requiring a cooldown period before it can be used again.

## Recipe Guide

| Ingredients | Resulting Beverage |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Coffee** + **Milk** | **Milk Coffee** |
| **Coffee** + **Water** | **Black Coffee** |
| **Espresso** + **Milk** | **Latte** |
| **Espresso** + **Water** | **Espresso** |

Enjoy your drink!
