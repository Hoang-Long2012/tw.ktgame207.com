## Item Overview

The **Bicycle** is an essential tool for rapid traversal across long distances. While it significantly increases your travel speed, be mindful that it consumes stamina more quickly than walking.

## Usage Instructions

To operate your bicycle effectively, follow these steps:

1.  **Retrieve from Inventory**: Open your backpack and equip the **Bicycle** to one of your hands.
2.  **Mounting**: Navigate to the hand holding the bicycle and press **Space** to mount it.
3.  **Movement**: 
    - Use the **Up Arrow** to move forward.
    - Use the **Down Arrow** to move backward.
4.  **Dismounting**: If you wish to stop riding, press **Alt + Backspace** to dismount and return to on-foot travel.

> [!NOTE]
> The bicycle is designed for linear movement. To change direction (turn left, right, or reverse), you must first orient your character toward the desired cardinal direction before moving. For instance, to turn left, face **West** and then press the **Up Arrow** to proceed forward in that direction.

Safe travels and good luck!
