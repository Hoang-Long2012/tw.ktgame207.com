## How to Sleep:

After exploring, hunting, fishing, cooking, and delivering fish, do you feel exhausted? It's time to rest!

## all step to sleep:

1. Retrieve your `hammock` from your backpack.
2. Press **Space** to deploy it.
3. Press **Q** to lie down on the hammock.
4. Press **Shift + Q** to start sleeping and recover your energy.
