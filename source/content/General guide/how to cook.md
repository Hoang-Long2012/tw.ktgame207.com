## How to Cook:

To cook food, you must first start a fire:

## all step:

1. **Start a Fire:** Currently, the game only supports starting fires using a `fire_starter`. Hold `fire_starter` in one of your hands and press **Space** to ignite a fire.
2. **Maintain the Fire:** Once the fire is lit, you must add wood to maintain it. Open your backpack, find `wood`, and press **Backspace** to drop it into the fire.
3. **Prepare the Meat:**
    - Hold the harvested animal carcass in one hand and a `knife` in the other.
    - Press **T** to butcher the carcass. Larger animals will require multiple butcher attempts.
    - Open your backpack, find the skinned animal carcass (`skinned_carcass`), hold it, and butchering it a second time with the knife to obtain raw meat ready for cooking.
4. **Put Meat in Skillet:** Hold the butchered raw meat in one hand and your `skillet` (pan) in the other, then press **T** to place the meat into the skillet.
5. **Cook the Meat:** Hold the skillet containing the raw meat in your hand, stand directly on the fire, and press **Space** to cook it.
6. **Harvest the Cooked Meat:** Once cooked, open your backpack, find the skillet containing the cooked meat, hold it in your hand, and press **T** to extract the cooked meat from the skillet.
