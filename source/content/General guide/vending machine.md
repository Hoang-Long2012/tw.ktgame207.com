**This is a vending machine system for a fee (player's coins). Each item sold, is of a different denomination. Most of the items sold are reasonably priced, not too expensive.
But how to use this system**?

## Things to prepare:

Before buying the item you need, the following steps will help you get what you need:

1. Go find the nearest vending machine.
2. In your the bag there will be coins. If not, search for it in many different ways.

## Usage:

The following will be how to use the vending machine. I'll help you through each step.

1. Please stand at the vending machine.
2. after, open your bag and hold the coin in your hand.
3. press **space** to open Insert coin Dialog.
4. Enter the number of coins you need to load into the machine in the box Amount.
5. press Insert button to put coins into the machine. cancel button to close dialog.
6. Next, press enter on the vending machine to open the purchasing interface.
7. Then you choose the item from Items List.
8. Then, press enter on each item or buy button. Here, you will be taken to a new interface.
9. Next, enter the number of items you want to buy at Enter amount to buy.
10. After entering, press enter. At this point, the machine will take the goods out for you and all that's left for you to do is put it in the bag.

**This is just the beginning**.

## Additional instructions about vending machine:

Here, will be the rest of the vending machine:

- Insert Money Button: As I mentioned above, you can click this button to load coins into the machine.
- Check Money In Machine Button: You can check in the machine to see how many coins.
- Withdraw Money Button: You can withdraw the remaining coins in the machine.
- Cancel Button: Close the purchase interface.
