## ticket system:

The ticket creation system helps us report errors to developers anywhere, anytime.
in addition, you can also suggest novel, interesting ideas to improve the game.

## create ticket:

Below are the steps for you to create a ticket or suggest a new idea:
1. press f8 to open option.
2. Press the down arrow to select support and contact and press enter.
3. press enter at create a support ticket.
4. in the first input box: please enter a subject for your ticket: Please enter a title for this ticket.
5. in the next input box: Please describe your problem in detail and clearly. Please let us know about your ideas or other suggestions.
6. after type, press tab and checked at: I am aware that if my support ticket is misleading or insulting, I will be banned from the game.
7. final press enter at send message Button to send ticket.

## report a bug:

Here, you can report the error you are encountering:

1. press f8 to open option.
2. Press the down arrow to select report a bug and press enter.
3. press enter at report a bug.
4. in the first input box: please enter a subject for your raport: Please write down the title so we can understand it clearly.
5. in the next input box: please write the bug you want to report in detail: Please explain your problem in detail.
6. Next, please checked: I am aware that if my bug report is misleading or insulting, I will be banned from the game.
7. Finally, press enter at the button send message to send to us.
