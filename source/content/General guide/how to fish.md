## How to Fish:

In your free time, is fishing your forte?
The following guide will be dedicated to that.

## Main steps:

1. Travel to a body of water (such as a river, lake, or sea).
2. Hold your `net` in one of your hands, switch to that hand, and press **Space** repeatedly until you hear a sharp **clicking sound**.
3. This clicking sound indicates that a fish has been caught in the net.
4. Open your backpack (**I**), locate `fish_in_net`, hold it in one of your hands, and press **T** to extract the fish from the net.
5. *Warning:* You must retrieve the fish quickly, or your net will tear!
6. Additionally, you can dive underwater (using **Page Down**) to search for delicious oysters.
