## How to Hunt:

After touring the prominent landmarks of TW, it is time to go hunting, fishing, and cook your very first meal!

## Steps to do:

1. Travel to an area inhabited by animals (such as the Island or the dark forest).
2. Hold a weapon in one of your hands, switch to that hand, and press **Space** to attack.
3. **If using firearms:**
    - Press **R** to reload ammunition.
    - Press **Shift + R** to unload ammunition.
    - Press **Alt + \`** to check the remaining ammo in the gun.
4. *Note:* If you cannot attack, you are likely in the **Peace State**. Press **Shift + F2** to switch to the **War State**.
5. Once you have successfully killed an animal, press **M** to open the map items menu, and navigate using the arrow keys to locate the carcass.
6. Press **Enter** on the carcass, press **N** to check how many steps away it is, walk to its position, and press **Enter** to harvest it into your backpack.
